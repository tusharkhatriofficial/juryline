# 3-Minute "Juryline" Demo Script

This script is designed to showcase the platform's core value: **efficiency, transparency, and AI-enhancement** for hackathon judging.

**Prerequisites:**
- Database seeded (`python db/seed.py`)
- Logged in as **Organizer** (`hello@tusharkhatri.in`) in one tab.
- Logged in as **Judge** (`khatritushar420@gmail.com`) in an Incognito window.

---

### Phase 1: The Problem & Context (0:00 - 0:30)
*Action: Start on the **Landing Page**.*

**"Hackathon judging is broken. It's usually a mess of spreadsheets, lost links, and tired judges. Juryline fixes this by streamlining the entire process."**

*Action: Click **"Go to Dashboard"** (Organizer View).*

**"As an organizer, I have a command center. I can see my active event, 'Global AI Hackathon 2026', right here with a beautiful custom banner."**

### Phase 2: The Organizer Experience (0:30 - 1:00)
*Action: Click on **"Global AI Hackathon 2026"**.*

**"Setting up is instant. I have my form builder, my criteria, and my judges all in one place."**

*Action: Click **"Submissions"** tab.*

**"I can see all 20 submissions instantly. But the real magic happens when I invite judges."** [SHOW the invite feature]

*Action: Click **"Judges"** tab -> Show 'Auto-Assign' button.*

**"I simply click the 'Auto-Assign' button, and Archestra's AI Agents instantly distribute the workload. It intelligently balances assignments so no judge is overwhelmed."**

### Phase 3: The Judge Experience (1:00 - 2:00)
*Action: **Switch to Incognito Window** (Judge View).*

**"Now, let's look at this from a Judge's perspective. No more spreadsheets."**

*Action: Click on **"Global AI Hackathon 2026"** card.*

**"I have my own personal queue. Archestra's Ingestion Agent has already validated these submissions, so I know I'm only reviewing valid projects."**

*Action: Click on **"EcoScan"** (First submission).*

**"This is the review interface. On the left, I have the project details and—crucially—the demo video embedded right here."**

*Action: **Play the video** for 5 seconds.*

**"I don't have to leave the tab. I watch, I read, and then I score."**

*Action: **Scroll to Rubric** on the right.*

**"The rubric is right here. I drag the sliders to score Innovation and Technical Complexity."**

*Action: Click **"Submit Review"**.*

**"Done. It auto-advances me to the next project. It's fast, focused, and frictionless."**

### Phase 4: The Results & Closing (2:00 - 3:00)
*Action: **Switch back to Organizer View**.*

*Action: Click **"Overview"** tab (Refresh if needed).*

**"Back on the organizer side, I see the results coming in real-time. The leaderboard updates automatically, calculating weighted averages so I can announce winners with confidence."**

*Action: Point to the **Leaderboard**.*

**"Juryline, powered by Archestra, turns hours of admin work into a simple, collaborative workflow."**

**"That is Juryline."**
